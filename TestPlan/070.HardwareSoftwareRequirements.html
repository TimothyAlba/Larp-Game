Hardware / Software Requirements

  • Windows
    • Processors: Intel Pentium 4, Intel Centrino, Intel Xeon, or Intel Core Duo (or compatible) 1.8 GHz minimum
    • Operating System: Microsoft Windows XP with Service Pack 2 or Windows Vista Home Premium, Business, Ultimate, or Enterpris
    • Memory: 512 MB of RA
    • Disk space: 256 MB of free disk space
    • Display: 1280x1024 resolution
    • Peripherals: Mouse and Keyboard
  • Mac
    • Processors: Dual-Core Intel, PowerPC G5
    • Operating System: Macintosh OS X 10.4.10 minimum
    • Memory: 512 MB of RAM
    • Disk space: 256 MB of free disk space
    • Peripherals: Mouse and Keyboard
