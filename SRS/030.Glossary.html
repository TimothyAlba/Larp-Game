Glossary

  • RPG - Role Playing Game where a player controls a character through a world filled with items, enemies, and precious loot.
  • Player - The user that is interacting with the software.
  • Knight - The character in the game that is controlled by the player.
  • Dungeon - The world the player will be interacting with that contains many rooms.
  • Room - An area within the dungeon that will contain chests, doors, and possibly enemies.
  • Chest - An object within a room that will contain an item(s) a player can add to their inventory.
  • Door - An area within the room that will allow the player to enter another room.
  • Consumable - An item in the player's inventory that will give a health boost or some other benefit to the player's knight and will be removed from inventory after a single use.
  • Play Game - An option in the opening screen that allows the player to start the game.
  • Inventory - An object in the game that contains the player's consumables and weapons.
  • Enemy - The characters the player will battle in the rooms.
  • Boss - The final enemy the player will battle to complete the game.
  • Run Away - An option during the battle stage where the player has a chance of leaving combat.
