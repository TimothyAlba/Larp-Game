User Requirements

  1. Player can select “Play Game” to begin a new game
  2. Player will be able to control the actions of a Knight who can
    1. move around within the boundaries of the room
    2. access inventory to use consumables and switch weapons
    3. interact with static world objects such as doors and chests
  3. Player’s Knight will encounter enemies in rooms, triggering combat phase where the Knight
    1. attack the enemy
    2. can heal
    3. run away from combat
  4. Player can end the game by
    1. finding and defeating the dungeon’s boss in combat
    2. letting their Knight’s hit points drop below 1
    3. quitting out of the game
