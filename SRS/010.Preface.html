SRS Preface

The purpose of this SRS is the layout the functional and non-functional requirements for team LARP’s RPG style game. Its goal is to help the developers better understand the software the team will be developing. The doccument is intended for devoloper use only.

It will also help any other readers better understand the software’s purpose.

The developers for this software are Chase Kiefer, Tim Lloyd, Tyree Gustafson, and Andrew Poss.
